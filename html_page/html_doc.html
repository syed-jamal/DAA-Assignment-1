Measure Algorithm

The measure algorithm first divides the 2d plane into stripes which are defined by horizontal edges of rectangles. Then each contribution from a stripe towards the area is calculated followed by summing them up. It uses the divide and conquer strategy with linear time taken to conquer. Hence the running time is nlogn.

Contour Algorithm

This algorithm also uses stripes for finding the contour of a set of rectangels in the plane. The intersection of the free region of a stripe with respect to a horizontal edge of a rectangle and the edge itself gives us the contribution for that edge. Our implementation finds the stripes in nlogn time and then finds the contour in quadratic time.

Assumption: No two rectangles have edges in alignment(y coordinates are distinct).

Input Format:-

x1 x2 y1 y2 (n lines)
Here n is the number of rectangles and (x1,y1) and (x2,y2) are the bottom left and top right corner points of a rectangle respectively.
